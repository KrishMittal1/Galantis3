Training

Orbital Passenger Training: Suit

The first day of training involves spacesuit fitting as well as underwater training. Underwater Training helps the passenger to get the feel for the suit as well as its limitations.

Orbital Passenger Training: Gimbal

The Gimbal is a tri-axis rotating machine used to test a person's motion-sickness. Those who suffer from motion-sickness, vertigo, or have a fear of heights are strongly advised to not travel.

Orbital Passenger Training: Instrumentation

Instrumentation is crucial to understand in order to have a safe launch as well as landing. A Majority of Day 3 will be spent on learning the instrumentation amongst all passengers so any passenger could take up the role as pilot and co-pilot.
