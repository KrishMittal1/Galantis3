Frequently Asked Questions

Why Space Travel?
Space Travel is a fascinating, as there are other worlds and other attributes to our universe that are undiscoverable on Earth. Our goal is to invite you on a once in a lifetime experience to witness a miracle outside of your comfort zone.On this trip you will see space
What are the trips that people will take?
As a new space travel agency, we are only focused on moon, mars, and orbit of earth trips. We are planning to expand in the next 10 years, but currently our services are limited to three different trips.
In preparation of the travel, how do you prepare the tourists?
As space traveling is fairly new and complicated, training is provided to the trainees. This includes zero gravity training, and training in tight spaces to easily adapt to the travel. This training isfairly safe, but will require you to sign waivers to partake in.
How many people can sign up for one trip?
As a new space travel space agency, we are only allowing groups of three to sign up at one time. The waiting process takes 1-2 months to accept a group. We take four groups at a time for both the trip and the training. We do train a fifth group just in case one group decides to drop out.
Approximately, how long is one trip?
Barring any complications, the trip should take 5 days with a 1 day launch and recovery time. This is to ensure the safety of all of the passengers and crew members within the vehicle. The trip will ensure that everyone's health is monitored, and will alert the whole vehicle if anything is out of the ordinary?
Will I enjoy the trip?
We can't speak for everyone, but we have recieved great feedback from the tourists that have already travelled with us. We have recieved feedback that we have used to improve the trip, as we are adapting to the complications we have already dealt with. Overall, the trip is for travel enthusiasts that are looking to do something out of the ordinary. If this sounds like you, we would love for you to hop aboard!
